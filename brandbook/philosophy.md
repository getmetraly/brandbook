# Philosophy

Metraly exists to empower engineering teams with **transparent, extensible analytics** on their own infrastructure.  Traditional SaaS engineering intelligence tools require organisations to send their source code and project data to a vendor’s cloud.  By contrast, Metraly is an **open‑core** platform designed for self‑hosting: your data stays in your PostgreSQL/TimescaleDB and Redis instances, on your servers【632635575803481†L24-L27】.  The core principles behind the brand are:

1. **Data ownership & privacy.**  All data lives within the customer’s environment.  The platform is built with compliance in mind and does not mandate vendor lock‑in【632635575803481†L24-L47】.
2. **Extensibility.**  Users can build custom plugins, dashboards and data sources through a planned plugin architecture; every calculation is auditable and open to modification【632635575803481†L30-L40】.
3. **AI on your terms.**  Future AI features are designed to run locally against your data.  Organisations can bring their own LLM or use built‑in lightweight models, keeping sensitive information private【632635575803481†L31-L36】.
4. **Engineering‑first identity.**  The visual language draws from observability aesthetics – pulse waves, telemetry lines, angular dashboards and real‑time signal patterns – to evoke a feeling of performance and control.  This identity is carried through every product surface, with a clean, systems‑oriented UI and minimal marketing gloss.
5. **Open collaboration.**  As an open‑core project, Metraly invites contribution and inspection.  The AGPL licence encourages improvements to be shared with the community while protecting the project’s long‑term viability.

6. **Enterprise readiness & global reach.**  Metraly is built to scale from startups to large enterprises.  Beyond self‑hosting, the platform is designed to support complex team structures, high data volumes and stringent governance.  It also embraces internationalisation, ensuring that the UI can adapt to multiple languages, scripts and cultural conventions without sacrificing usability.

## Tagline

> **Your data, your AI, your servers — by design, not as an afterthought.**【632635575803481†L2-L7】

The tagline encapsulates Metraly’s promise: to give engineering teams control over their telemetry and insights without sacrificing privacy or flexibility.

## Tone of Voice

- **Engineering‑driven:** speak like a seasoned engineer.  Prioritise clarity over marketing fluff.  Use precise language and avoid exaggeration.
- **Transparent:** be open about what the product does and doesn’t do.  Highlight the limitations alongside the capabilities.
- **Confidence without arrogance:** Metraly solves complex problems but remains humble.  Let the results speak for themselves rather than resorting to hyperbole.
- **Inclusive:** write in a way that welcomes contributions from diverse backgrounds and experience levels.

This philosophy underpins every visual and written decision in the brandbook.
